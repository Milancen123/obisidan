# Sections System

## Purpose
Sections are full page blocks (hero, features, footer).
They combine layout, spacing, typography, and components.

---

## Base Structure (**always use**)

<section>
    padding-section
	    container-main
		    content
</section>

---

## section-hero

### Layout
- flex-column (heading, paragraph, button)

### Structure
<section>
	padding-section
		container-main
				flex-column
					
</section>


### Notes
- Switch to flex-column on mobile
- Center align on mobile
